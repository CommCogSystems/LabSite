Emma Hite

Graphic design assistant


I’m a 4th year undergraduate visual communication design major working on various projects with Dr. Coronel and graduate students in the lab. In addition to designing and developing the lab website, I’ve created vector graphics used in scientific figures and photographic images used in eye tracking studies. Outside of the lab, my work explores how design can help create a more socially and environmentally responsible world. Check out my portfolio here: emmahitedesign.wixsite.com/portfolio
