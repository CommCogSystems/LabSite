Iris Blackburn

Undergraduate research assistant


My name is Iris Blackburn and I am a first year majoring in Linguistics at The Ohio State University. I joined Professor Coronel’s Lab during my senior year of high school and have been co-authoring an op-ed focusing on informing the general audience of how eye-tracking’s benefits will become prevalent in the future and how we should keep the risks to a minimum and benefits to a maximum. I have compiled and read news and scientific articles related to eye-tracking technology and proposed legislation governing user agreements related to digital trace data or bio-sensor data.
