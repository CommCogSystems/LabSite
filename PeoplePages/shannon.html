Shannon Poulsen

Shannon Poulsen is a Masters of Arts student in the School of Communication at Ohio State University. Her research interests include political misinformation, biased message processing, and information recall. Specifically, she is interested in finding ways to mitigate the effects of motivated reasoning in order to increase consideration of factually-supported information. Her research in this lab concerns how misinformation spreads through social interaction and the potential consequences of such actions.
