Jared Ott

I am a 1st year PhD student in the Communication program at Ohio State. My research interests, most broadly, lie in understanding how beliefs and attitudes are formed and modified. I have most recently been looking into how narrative persuasion and self-affirmation play a role in these processes. I am also collaborating on a project to assess the impact of social interaction on message framing. When not working on coursework or research, you can most likely find me chasing my 2 and 5-year old children around.
