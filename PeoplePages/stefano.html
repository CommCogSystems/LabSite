Stefano Todaro

My name is Stefano Todaro and I'm a junior majoring in strategic communication. Along with my major, I am minoring in professional writing and film studies. I'm passionate about writing and also about my movies, so I thought it would be a good idea to do those two minors. I am first and foremost a creative person, and I hope to get a job at an ad agency after graduation. When it comes to research I'm most interested in the effects advertising has on people. I'm also interested in research involving music and politics, as those are two other things that are important to me.
